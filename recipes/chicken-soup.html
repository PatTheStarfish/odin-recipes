Chicken soup

Description

A good way of staying healthy

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken parts
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Water
  1. Put carrots, celery, chicken parts into a pot
  2. Fill pot with water and bring to a boil
  3. Leave it to slowly boil for an hour
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Serve and enjoy
